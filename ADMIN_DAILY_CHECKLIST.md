# Admin Daily Checklist

- [ ] **Dashboard Review**: Check "Pending Orders" and "Low Stock" alerts on the main dashboard.
- [ ] **Design Sync**: Verify that the Google Drive auto-sync ran successfully (check `Settings > Drive Sync`).
- [ ] **Pending Information**: Clear at least 5 items from the `Design Management > Pending Info` queue.
- [ ] **WhatsApp**: Check for any failed message deliveries in `Marketing > WhatsApp > Logs`.
- [ ] **Order Status**: Ensure all "Dispatched" orders have valid tracking numbers updated.
