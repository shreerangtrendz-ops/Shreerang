# Admin Guide: Accounting & Fabric Master

## Fabric Master Management
*   **Organization**: Use the new Dashboard to view fabrics grouped by type.
*   **Favorites**: Click the Star icon on frequently used fabrics to keep them at the top of lists.
*   **Bulk Delete**: Use the checkbox selection to delete multiple obsolete records at once.
*   **Filters**: Use the "Advanced Filters" panel to find fabrics by width, process, or status.

## Purchase Bill Management
*   **Entry**: Go to `Accounting > Purchase Bills`. Click "Add Bill".
*   **AI Import**: Use "Bulk Import" to upload images of bills. The system will try to read the Vendor, Date, and Amount.
*   **Brokerage**: If a broker was involved, enter the brokerage % to track that expense separately.

## Job Work Bill Management
*   **Entry**: Go to `Accounting > Job Work Bills`.
*   **Specificity**: Always link a Job Work bill to a **Design Number** for accurate product costing.
*   **Workers**: Ensure the Job Worker is selected correctly; different workers have different rates.

## Sales Bill Management
*   **Entry**: Go to `Accounting > Sales Bills`.
*   **Commissions**: If an Agent is linked to the customer, enter the commission % to track liability.

## Pending Order Management
*   **Workflow**: Do not manually edit quantities if possible. Use the **Dispatch** button.
*   **Tracking**: This module is your "Order Book". Check it daily to see what needs to be shipped.
*   **Cleanup**: Use Bulk Delete to remove cancelled orders.

## Commission/Brokerage Management
*   **Overview**: This module acts as a ledger for your agents and brokers.
*   **Entry**: Entries here are often auto-created from Sales/Purchase bills but can be added manually for adjustments.
