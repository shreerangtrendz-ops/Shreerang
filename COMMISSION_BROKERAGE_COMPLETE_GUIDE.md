# Commission & Brokerage Guide

## Purpose
Tracks liabilities to third-party agents (Commission on Sales) and brokers (Brokerage on Purchases).

## Automation
*   **Sales Bills**: If a commission % is entered, an entry appears here.
*   **Purchase Bills**: If a brokerage % is entered, an entry appears here.

## Manual Entry
You can manually add entries for:
*   Bonuses.
*   Adjustments.
*   Flat-fee commissions not tied to % of a bill.

## Reporting
Use the filters to generate a monthly statement for a specific Agent to process their payout.
