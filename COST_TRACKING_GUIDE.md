# Cost Tracking Guide

## Dashboard Overview
The Cost Tracking Dashboard gives you a financial view of your fabric operations.

## Metrics
*   **Total Expenditure**: Sum of all confirmed bills.
*   **Raw Material**: Cost of Base Fabric purchases.
*   **Job Work**: Cost of processing (dyeing, printing, embroidery).

## Analysis
*   **Pie Charts**: Visualize where your money is going (Supplier vs Job Worker).
*   **Bill History**: A searchable list of all imported bills with status.

## Best Practices
*   Import bills immediately upon receipt.
*   Verify the "Mapped Fabric" to ensure accurate product costing.
