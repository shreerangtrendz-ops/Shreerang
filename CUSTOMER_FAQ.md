# Customer FAQ (WhatsApp)

**Q: How do I order?**
A: Simply reply with the Design Number and Quantity. Example: "Order D-101 50 meters".

**Q: How do I see new designs?**
A: Type "Show designs" or specify a category like "Show Rayon".

**Q: Can I track my order?**
A: Yes, type "Status" followed by your Order ID.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept Bank Transfer and UPI. Payment details will be sent upon order confirmation.
