# Design Management Guide

## Purpose
Manage the visual catalog of your fabrics.

## Features
*   **Bulk Upload**: Upload 50+ images at once.
*   **Auto-Naming**: Filenames are used as Design Numbers automatically.
*   **Linking**: Assign designs to Finish Fabrics for accurate inventory tracking.

## Workflow
1.  Navigate to `Design Manager`.
2.  Click `Bulk Upload`.
3.  Drag and drop images.
4.  Select a "Default Fabric" if all designs belong to one type (e.g., "Cotton Print").
5.  Save.
