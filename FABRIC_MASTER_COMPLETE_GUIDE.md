# Fabric Master Complete Guide

## Overview
The Fabric Master module is the heart of your inventory management, handling the entire lifecycle of fabric from raw material to finished goods.

## Key Components

### 1. Base Fabric Master
*   **Purpose**: Manage raw/grey fabrics.
*   **Key Fields**: Width, GSM, Weight, HSN Code.
*   **Features**: Stock toggles, Supplier linking.

### 2. Finish Fabric Master
*   **Purpose**: Manage processed fabrics (dyed, printed).
*   **Features**: Link to Base Fabric, Design Upload, Process Type.

### 3. Fancy Finish Master
*   **Purpose**: Manage value-added fabrics (embroidery, etc.).
*   **Features**: Costing per meter, Job Worker linking.

## Visibility Settings
Use the **Category Visibility** toggles at the top of the main dashboard to hide sections you don't use regularly. This keeps your workspace clean.

## Stock Management
*   **Ready Stock**: Mark items as available for immediate shipment.
*   **Out of Stock**: Keep items in the catalog but mark them unavailable.
*   Use the **Bulk Action Toolbar** to update stock status for multiple items at once.

## Cost Tracking
The system now includes a comprehensive cost tracking module.
1.  **Import Bills**: Use the Bulk Bill Import tool to upload physical bills.
2.  **Auto-Map**: The AI will suggest which fabric record the bill belongs to.
3.  **Track**: View the Cost Tracking Dashboard to see expenditure breakdown.
