# HSN Code Master Setup Guide

## PART 1: INITIAL SETUP
To begin setting up your accounting codes:
1.  Navigate to **Admin > HSN Master** (via the sidebar).
2.  You will see tabs for **Process**, **Value Addition**, **Garments**, and **Expenses**.
3.  This central dashboard controls the HSN codes available in dropdowns throughout the system.

## PART 2: BASE FABRIC HSN CODES
*These are entered directly in the **Fabric Master > Base Fabric** form, but here are standard codes to reference:*

*   **5007**: Woven fabrics of silk or silk waste.
*   **5208**: Woven fabrics of cotton, containing 85% or more by weight of cotton, weighing not more than 200 g/m2.
*   **5209**: Woven fabrics of cotton, containing 85% or more by weight of cotton, weighing more than 200 g/m2.
*   **5407**: Woven fabrics of synthetic filament yarn (Polyester).
*   **5512**: Woven fabrics of synthetic staple fibers, containing 85% or more by weight of such fibers.
*   **5513**: Woven fabrics of synthetic staple fibers, containing less than 85% by weight, mixed mainly with cotton.

## PART 3: PROCESS HSN CODES
*Setup these in the **Process HSN** tab for finishing job work.*

1.  Click **Add Process HSN**.
2.  **Process Name**: e.g., "Printing" or "Digital Print".
3.  **HSN Code**: e.g., **9988** (Job work services) or **5209** (if billed as manufacturing).
4.  **GST Rate**: Typically **5%** for textile job work.
5.  **Status**: Active.

## PART 4: VALUE ADDITION HSN CODES
*Setup these in the **Value Addition** tab for fancy work.*

1.  Click **Add Value Addition HSN**.
2.  **Value Addition Name**: e.g., "Embroidery".
3.  **HSN Code**: e.g., **5810** (Embroidery in the piece, in strips or in motifs).
4.  **GST Rate**: Typically **12%** (or 5% depending on specific notification).
5.  **Status**: Active.

## PART 5: EXPENSE HSN CODES
*Setup these in the **Expenses** tab for overheads.*

1.  Click **Add Expense HSN**.
2.  **Expense Name**: e.g., "Packaging Material".
3.  **HSN Code**: **4819** (Cartons, boxes).
4.  **GST Rate**: **12%** or **18%**.
5.  **Status**: Active.

## PART 6: READYMADE GARMENT HSN CODES
*Setup these in the **Garments** tab for finished goods sales.*

1.  Click **Add Garment HSN**.
2.  **Garment Name**: e.g., "Kurtis" or "Women's Suits".
3.  **HSN Code**: **6204** (Women's or girls' suits, ensembles, jackets, blazers, dresses, skirts, divided skirts, trousers).
4.  **GST Rate**:
    *   **5%** (If sale value ≤ ₹1000).
    *   **12%** (If sale value > ₹1000).
    *   *Note: You may need two entries if you sell in both price brackets.*
5.  **Status**: Active.
