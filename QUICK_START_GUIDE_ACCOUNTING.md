# Quick Start Guide: Accounting Setup

## Step 1: Setup Fabric Master
1.  Navigate to `Fabric Master`.
2.  Create your **Base Fabrics** (Raw materials like Cotton, Rayon).
3.  Create **Finish Fabrics** (Processes like Print, Dye) linked to Base.
4.  Create **Fancy Finish** (Value added like Embroidery) linked to Finish.
5.  *Tip: Star your top 10 fabrics for easy access.*

## Step 2: Add People
1.  Go to `People > Suppliers` and add your fabric vendors.
2.  Go to `People > Job Workers` and add your dyers/printers.

## Step 3: Start Billing (Purchase)
1.  Go to `Accounting > Purchase Bills`.
2.  Add your first bill manually to understand the fields.
3.  Try the **Bulk Import** with a photo of a bill to test the AI.

## Step 4: Manage Orders
1.  Go to `Accounting > Pending Orders`.
2.  Create a dummy order.
3.  Click the **Truck Icon (Dispatch)** to simulate sending goods.
4.  Check `Sales Bills` to see the auto-generated invoice.

## Step 5: Track Job Work
1.  Go to `Accounting > Job Work Bills`.
2.  Enter a bill for a specific process (e.g., "Digital Print").
3.  Link it to a specific Design Number.

## Step 6: Review Costing
1.  Go to `Cost Tracking` (in Fabric Master or Accounting).
2.  Check how your expenses are distributed between Material and Job Work.
