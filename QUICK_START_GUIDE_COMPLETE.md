# Quick Start Guide

## Step 1: Initial Setup
1.  **HSN Master**: Go to `Admin > HSN Master`. Ensure common codes (5208, 9988, etc.) are added.
2.  **People**: Go to `Admin > People`. Add at least one **Supplier** and one **Job Worker**.

## Step 2: Create Your First Fabric
1.  Navigate to `Fabric Master > Base Fabric`.
2.  Click **Add New**.
3.  Fill in **Fabric Name** (e.g., "60x60 Cotton").
4.  Select **HSN Code** (Compulsory).
5.  (Optional) Select a **Supplier** and **Cost**.
6.  Click **Save**.

## Step 3: Add a Finish
1.  Navigate to `Fabric Master > Finish Fabric`.
2.  Click **Add New**.
3.  Select the **Base Fabric** you just created.
4.  Select a **Process** (e.g., "Digital Print").
5.  Notice that HSN Codes auto-populate.
6.  **Upload a Design Image** (optional but recommended).
7.  Click **Save**.

## Step 4: Manage Stock
1.  Go to the `Fabric Master` dashboard.
2.  Locate your fabric in the list.
3.  Use the **Ready Stock** toggle to mark it as available.
4.  If it runs out, simply toggle to **Out of Stock** (no need to delete).

## Step 5: Import Bills (Accounting)
1.  Go to `Fabric Master > Bulk Bill Import`.
2.  Upload a photo of a purchase invoice.
3.  Let AI extract the data.
4.  **Map** it to the specific Fabric Record.
5.  **Confirm**. The cost is now logged in the `Cost Tracking` dashboard.
