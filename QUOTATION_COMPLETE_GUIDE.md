# Quotation Module Guide

## Purpose
A staging area for potential costs. Store rates from suppliers before they become actual bills.

## Workflow
1.  **Create**: Enter quote details (Supplier, Rate, Item).
2.  **Compare**: Use the list view to compare quotes from different vendors.
3.  **Convert**: When you decide to buy/proceed, click the **arrow icon** to Convert to Bill.
    *   The system creates a Purchase/Job Work bill.
    *   The Quotation is archived as "Converted".

## Benefits
*   Keeps your Bill ledger clean of "potential" costs.
*   Provides a history of price trends (Quoted vs. Actual).
