# Sales Bill Module Guide

## Purpose
Records invoices issued to customers. This is your Revenue module.

## Key Features
*   **Commission Tracking**: Calculates amount owed to sales agents.
*   **Auto-Generation**: Can be created automatically from the Pending Orders dispatch flow.

## How to Add (Manual)
1.  Click **"Add Sales Bill"**.
2.  Select **Customer**.
3.  Enter **Item Name** and **Quantity**.
4.  Enter **Rate** (Sales Price).
5.  (Optional) Enter **Commission %**.
6.  Save.

## Best Practices
*   Ideally, generate these via the **Pending Orders Dispatch** workflow to ensure order balances are updated correctly.
*   Use manual entry only for direct sales without prior orders.
