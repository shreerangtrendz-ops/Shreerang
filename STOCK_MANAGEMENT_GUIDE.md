# Stock Management Guide

## Quick Stock Toggles
In every fabric form (Base, Finish, Fancy), you will now find a "Stock Status" section.

*   **Ready Stock**: Enable this if the goods are physically available in the warehouse for immediate dispatch.
*   **Out of Stock**: Enable this to flag the item as unavailable in the catalog without deleting it.

## Best Practices
1.  **Regular Updates**: Use the "Bulk Action" toolbar in the dashboard lists to update stock status for multiple items at once.
2.  **Filtering**: Use the dashboard filters to view only "Active" or "Ready Stock" items.
