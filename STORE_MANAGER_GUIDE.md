# Store Manager Guide

## Daily Stock Updates
1.  Go to `Fabric Master`.
2.  Walk through the warehouse/shelves.
3.  For any item that is physically available, ensure the **Ready Stock** toggle is **ON** (Green).
4.  For items that are empty, toggle to **Out of Stock** (Grey).
5.  *Tip: You can filter the list by "Out of Stock" to see what needs replenishment.*

## New Design Arrival
1.  When new designs arrive, take photos.
2.  Go to the specific `Finish Fabric` record.
3.  Edit -> Upload the design image.
4.  Enter the **Design Number**.
5.  Save. This makes it visible to the sales team immediately.

## Checking Prices
*   If you need to know the cost of a fabric, check the **Cost Tracking** dashboard or the specific fabric record details (if permissions allow).
